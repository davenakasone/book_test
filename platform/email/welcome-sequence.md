# The Forbidden Bulletin — welcome sequence (5 emails)

Mechanics: email 0 fires on signup; then one every 3 days. Every email ends
with the same two lines: the satire disclaimer and a single CTA. One CTA
per email, ever. Subject lines under ~45 chars so they don't truncate on
phones.

---

### Ongoing cadence (post-sequence)
Weekly dispatch, rotating: (a) site dossier from a chapter, (b) checksum
challenge, (c) "mailbag" answering a real reader reply in voice, (d) launch
/ next-book news. Every fourth email re-plugs the book; the rest plug the
*next* email. The list's job is retention; the book sells on trust
accumulated between pitches.
